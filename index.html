Explaining the works of rocket ships

Why Rocket Ships are awesome!

  • Rockets are the only way humans can escape the boundaries of the earths atmosphere.
  • They allow us to explore the stars and discover new things in space.
  • They allow us to be able to push the boundaries of modern science.

How do they work?
  1. The bulk of the rocket is made up of storage for fuel to propel the rocket
  2. At the top of the rocket on top of the fuel tank there is usually a pointy shaped nose that helps the rocket's aerodynamics.
  3. At the base of the rocket lies the thruster, this mechanism burns the fuel from the tank which produces thrust

Conclusion

Overall, Rocket ships continue to be one of the most valuable human
creations ever.Without them we wouldn't be able to even leave the force field of the
atmosphere and explore the vast infinity of the stars.
